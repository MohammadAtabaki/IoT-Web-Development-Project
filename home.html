Tesla's Storm Watch

Storm Watch is a feature of Tesla's Powerwall that prepares homeowners for grid outages. Relying on internet connectivity, the Powerwall responds to severe weather alerts by allocating available grid and/or solar energy, ensuring the Powerwall get a sufficient charge to power the home during a storm.
